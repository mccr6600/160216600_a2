Professor: Alan Turing

Alan Turing was a mathmetician and computer scientist who was born on June 23, 1912 in London, UK. He has a PhD in Math from his time studying at Princeton University. He was a leading participant in the breaking of German ciphers during World War 2. Despite the fact he killed himself in 1954 Dr. Turing is excited to teach this class.
