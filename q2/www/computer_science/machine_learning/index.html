Professor: Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer who has been brought back to life to teach this course. She was born on January 16, 1816 in London. She had a long working relationship with Charles Babbage, known as the father of computers, and Ada herself created what many consider the first computer program.
